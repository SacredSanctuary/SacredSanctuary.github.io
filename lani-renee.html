Tarot Reading

A Tarot Reading begins the journey and it is when your book of life is opened and what is dormant, hidden, or waiting to be revealed surfaces; whether you have questions or not, the truth is always revealed. Lani is available for phone readings.

Life Wheel Reading

The Life Wheel Reading is a process of defining oneself through the discovery of specific archetypes that describe the twelve aspects of one’s individuality. It literally becomes a tool, once the archetypes are placed in the wheel that determines one’s journey. (It is highly recommended to then deepen your understanding of your life wheel through monthly readings with Lani Renee)

Healing

Healing is the work that is often required for an individual to become balanced, energetically aligned, and centered in order to support one’s progression.

Cord-Cutting

Cord-Cutting is an energetic healing modality that releases emotional ties, cords, and attachments that are no longer serving one’s soul or progression; for example, failed marriage, relationships, death, toxic friendships, painful childhood experiences, and any emotion one is ready to let go of. This healing is extremely empowering and liberating.

DNA Activation

DNA Activation is an etheric healing modality based on the ancient wisdom first formulated in the temple of King Solomon. The human DNA has 12 codons of physical and spiritual DNA strands that make up the double helix that is our internal stairway to heaven. The goal of the DNA Activation is to energetically unite the spiritual and physical DNA to heal and integrate with one’s highest potential. It is recommended to have a DNA Activation once every one to three months for the best progressive results.

DNA Reading

DNA Reading is a channeling from the eleventh codon revealing the record of one’s spiritual DNA and valuable information such as the origin of one’s spirit, information on past life emanations, one’s oneness with God, the archangelic self, one’s blue print for self-mastery, to a description of the higher self, and more! This reading is for those seeking true enlightenment of a greater self and are willing to apply this hidden knowledge to their personal alchemy.

Etheric Surgery

*Exorcisms

Exorcisms are a very sacred and holy process of releasing or depossessing dark spirits in a human soul. Due to the danger of this healing modality Lani Renee is very selective on whom she does an exorcism for. There are requirements to prepare one for an exorcism and additional work that must be carried through to ensure its success. Possession of a human soul is very misunderstood in a culture because of the overwhelming acceptance of human sin. If one is contributing to the darkness that is feeding off one’s soul, the lack of accountability guarantees failure. To need an exorcism requires humility and a willingness to change.

Lani’s regular rate does not apply to exorcisms. Cost will be determined per exorcism depending on the circumstances of the situation.
